GradList: Streamline Your Digital Colour Experience

GradList is an app that deals with colours, offering two distinctive functions:

  1. A colour converter for digital formats from hex to RGB.
  2. It produces a list of colours for programmers to use. Within that list, you can choose several options, including colour format, format of the list, gradient options between colours, and the number of steps for stop colours.

GradList is an essential tool for digital creatives, offering two key features:

  • Colour Conversion Made Simple: Quickly convert between HEX and RGB formats, ensuring compatibility and accuracy.
  • Customisable Colour Libraries: Create and customize colour lists with options for formats, list styles, and gradient steps. GradList is not just an app; it's your ally in digital design, making colour management effortless and inspiring.

Madyard

Description

Madyard is a plant business I helped to set up that sells plants from Madeira (my home town) and Canaries in the UK. My partner is very computer challenged, so the website had to be easy to update the never-ending ins and out of plant stock. I used Isotope JS to create a dynamic plant catalogue that you can sort and filter according to several categories.
